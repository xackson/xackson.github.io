Welcome

Hello. My name is Jackson Kindel. If you're reading this, then thank you for taking the time to visit. I built myself this website to act as an extension of myself; a playground for my antics both creative and technical. For now, this site will act as both a journal to write in, and a place for me to experiment with web-design.

I have this weird prediction that, after social media permeates every aspect of life, there will come a regression in terms of how we care to access each other online-- Twitter? A cesspool. Instagram? Best case, it lives until it turns into LinkedIn. ... LinkedIn? Straight in the garbage-- and then same with Instagram. By the time we've realized that social media is the energy-sucking nonsense that we've all known it to be, we will come full circle back to good ol' HTML and CSS. Evolution, baby.

... Anyways if/when that time comes, your true online presence will become the content that you have built with your own two hands-- I want to land on your silly little website and remember you forever. I want to wonder why your color pallette matches your aesthetic; I want to see the photos that are the most important to you, and why you felt so compelled to lay them out the way you did; I want to see your essays, whether satirical, philosophical, or downright ordinary. I want to see how you fluorish in the Wild West 3.0 of internet.

Anyways, the point I'm grasping at is that tech companies took it upon themselves to "give us a platform" meanwhile robbing us of the actual experience of the internet. A curated instagram page? No thanks. A highly reactive, jQuery-saturated, parallax-induced-dizziness of a landing page? Again, no thank you.

content warning

Let it be known: the words that I journal here are written by stream-of-consciousness; a reflection of the synaptic mindpower traveling from my brain and into my keyboard.

Some of the themes will come across as judgemental, vile, distasteful, and/or downright hurtful. While I will try to make some of the content as friendly as possible, I feel like I have to say that from the bottom of my heart: no, I'm not attacking you. When I write, I'm tackling demons that plague mine and (dare I say what seems like) our minds; staring at insecurties, projections, and toxicities right in their stupid faces and enabling them, so as to destroy them for good. My inspiration comes from within, and I'm here to let that dragon run amok.

My writing philosophy is that I don't write with the intention that anything I make will be read. I think it would be disingenuous of me if I did. If you leave judgement at the door, some things written might be found as "funny", but that is not my intention (or maybe it is. you be the judge). I'm not a hater, or some salty dog. I am no better (or worse) than you. I just need to be clear: this is entirely for my personal entertainment.

I implore anyone, at anytime, no matter what the occasion, to message me. Short or long, I don't mind. I have profound amounts of love in my heart for any person that has come/gone or is currently in my life. I am accessible, yet clearly afraid, excited, even...
