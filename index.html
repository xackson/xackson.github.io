Welcome to my GitHub Page. This is an impromptu project-portfolio that I will be expanding upon in the coming weeks/months.

Mind the cringe: the bulk of this site was used for a little webdev project for in of my past classes.
I am currently figuring the ins and outs of GitHub pages (and other extensions of it) in order to build a personal portfolio of arts/design of every dimension, including music, and essays.

Because I am building it completely from scratch, you will find that the site looks absolutely f*cked.

I am actively working on formatting the site to work on mobile, as all the code was originally formatted for computers only.

I am a student of many disciplines; I have grown up knowing that I am not destined to fit within just one segment of this life.

This website will be a place where I can pour myself out to everyone, as well as a place to show off bits of my technical knowledge...

About Me

My name is Jackson Kindel (he/him). I go by both "Jack" and "Jackson"

Now 25 years of age, I have since been a student at VCU since I was 19. I took a 3-semester hiatus after my freshman year to reevaluate my academic goals.

During my time away, I was a landscaper and a cellphone sales representative while living in Northern Virginia, and then a waiter working on Snowshoe Mountain in Slatyfork, West Virginia.

5 Things About Me

  1. I am passionate about music. I picked up the guitar at age 10 or 11 and have played ever since. I played in two bands in high school, and performed with a live band/ensemble for a theatrical production of "Hair! The Musical" at the end of my senior year in high school. I am currently seeking mildly-experienced musical partners in Richmond, Virginia, who want to pursue alternative/hardcore/black-metal/shoegaze/post-rock etc projects.

  2. I like skateboarding but I don't film nearly as much as I should (I feel like Max Palmer, sometimes...). I had a small line in a skateboarding video premier at Grace Street Theater in Winter 2022.

  3. I love geology and astronomy. I enjoy unearthing and collecting cool rocks. I have a moderately-nice telescope that I love to use on nights when the moon is bright, or when a certain planet is visible in the sky. I also collect trading cards; vintage cigarette cards, baseball, basketball, Magic: The Gathering, and Yu-gi-ho cards.

  4. I fucking love cooking on my cast-iron skillet.

  5. My music taste is incredibly broad and obscure; not consistent in the slightest. I am hoping to begin a temporary career in radio as a DJ at WRIR 97.3 FM this fall/winter.
