About This Page

I built myself this website so I wouldn't need to use Twitter, Tumblr, or Threads in order to worry about my thoughts never being heard. Plus, I really dislike the constant limitations imposed by other websites.

Let it be known: the words that are printed here are written by stream-of-consciousness; a reflection of the synaptic brainpower traveling from my mind and into my keyboard.

Among other things I want to bring into this space, this is a place for me to write. Many of these entries will come across as distasteful, judgemental, and downright hurtful. When I am writing, I am tackling the demons that plague mine and (dare I say, what seems like, evidently) our minds; staring at insecurties, projections, and toxcicities right in their stupid faces and enabling them, so as to destroy them for good.

If you leave judgement at the door, some things written might be found as funny, but that is not my intention. I'm not a salty dog, or a hater. I am no better (or worse) than you. I just want to be clear: this is entirely for my personal entertainment.

I implore anyone, at anytime, no matter what the occasion, to message me. Short or long, I don't mind. I have profound amounts of love in my heart for every person that has come/gone from my life. I am accessible, yet I am the one who is afraid.
